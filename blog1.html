Care Instructions

To ensure your nighty lasts, follow these care instructions:

  1. Machine wash on a gentle cycle with similar colors.
  2. Use a mild detergent and avoid bleach.
  3. Tumble dry on low heat or hang to dry.
  4. Iron on a low setting if needed.
