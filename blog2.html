Care Tips for Your Comfort Wears

To keep your Comfort Wears looking and feeling great, follow these care tips:

  1. Machine wash with cold water on a gentle cycle.
  2. Use mild detergents and avoid fabric softeners.
  3. Tumble dry on low or air dry to preserve fabric integrity.
  4. Iron on a low setting if needed, avoiding any printed areas.
