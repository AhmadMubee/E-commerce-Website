Care Instructions

Keep your t-shirt looking vibrant and fresh by following these care instructions:

  1. Machine wash cold with like colors.
  2. Use a mild detergent and avoid bleach.
  3. Tumble dry low or hang to dry.
  4. Iron on a low setting, avoiding printed areas.
